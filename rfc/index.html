YAML Specification RFCs

Changes to the YAML specification are made using an RFC process.

Current RFCs

  • RFC-0001 — Empty-scalars not allowed as keys
  • RFC-0002 — Documents after the first require a document-start-indicator
  • RFC-0003 — Only ASCII-word-characters and dash are allowed in anchor names
  • RFC-0004 — Maximum of 8 spaces per indentation level
  • RFC-0005 — Directives belong to the stream and not a document
  • RFC-0006 — Bare documents of flow collections may be separated by a newline
